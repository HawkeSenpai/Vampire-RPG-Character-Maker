The Timeline

  • The Blood Plague: Vampiric origin. Clots blood, kills thousands.
  • The Rituals: Desperate folk remedies attracted more vampires.
  • The Vanishing: The King and his retinue disappeared in Marchbirge Forest.
  • Martial Law: The Church seized power to organize defense.

The Church

Fanatic pragmatists. They burn collaborators and hoard food. They are not cruel, but cold. They will sacrifice a village to save the Kingdom.

Vampire Weaknesses

  • Sunlight: Instant death.
  • Silver: Acts like poison.
  • Invitation: Cannot enter without being invited.

Human Magic

  • Clergy: Slow rituals. Weak combat blessings.
  • Alchemy: Resource-heavy. Anyone can throw it, few can make it.
  • Forbidden: Uses vampire blood. Corrupting. Hunted by Church.
  • True Mages: 1 in 100,000. Powerful artifacts require lifetime investment.
